[About.]

Band Biography

As Time Fades is a pop punk band from Cleveland, Ohio. Formed in 2015, the goal has been to bring music to everyone by sharing stories that people can easily relate to. The band wants to be as accessible as possible, anyone should be able to say "hi" to one of the artists without feeling shy.

Daniel Dunlap - Lead vocals & rythym guitar

Daniel is the rhythm guitarist and lead vocalist for As Time Fades. He was born on March 1st, 1997. He has been playing guitar for about 5 years, but has been singing for the majority of his life. He mainly sang in highschool choirs until he joined As Time Fades in September of 2014. He enjoys singing, playing guitar, or anything to do with music. His inspiration comes from bands like Green Day, Neck Deep, and Mayday Parade.

Danny Kekedy - Lead guitar & backup vocals

Danny was born January 24th 1997. He has been playing guitar for 10 years, and his inspiration to play guitar at the beginning was Slash. He is currently working to get a degree in Electronic Engineering Technology, but is hoping to make music a full time job. Hobbies of his include soccer, acting, writing and playing music. He is influenced musically by classic rock and pop punk.

Schuyler Spivey - Bass guitar

Schuyler plays bass for As Time Fades. He has been playing bass for over 5 years and plays an assortment of other instruments.Currently, he is studying aerospace systems engineering at the University of Akron. He is also a part of the club rugby team at the University of Akron. Schuyler enjoys reading books, especially novels written by Stephen King. His music influence is deeply rooted in motown, funk, rock, and R&B; however, he listens to a wide range of music-- folk, rap, pop punk, indie, house, etc.

Toby Caulk - Drums

Toby plays drums for As Time Fades. He has been laying down beats for four years, although he started with violin and trombone. He loves anything creative and is always looking to expand upon his artistic abilities. One of Toby's main pursuits in life is to bring music to everyone, and drums are currently his best way to push that dream forward. He also enjoys capturing moments in the form of pictures, loves to fly, and is a professional software developer. His main influences are Chris Adler, Brann Dailor, and Dani Washington with roots mainly in metal and soft rock.
