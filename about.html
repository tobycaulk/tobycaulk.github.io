[About.]

As Time Fades is a pop punk band from Cleveland, Ohio. Formed in 2015, the goal has been to bring music to everyone by sharing stories that people can easily relate to. The band wants to be as accessible as possible, anyone should be able to say "hi" to one of the artists without feeling shy.
