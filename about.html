Band Biography

As Time Fades is a pop punk band from Cleveland, Ohio. Formed in 2015, the goal has been to bring music to everyone by sharing stories that people can easily relate to. The band wants to be as accessible as possible, anyone should be able to say "hi" to one of the artists without feeling shy.

Schuyler Spivey - Bass guitar

Schuyler plays bass for As Time Fades. He has been playing bass for over 5 years and plays an assortment of other instruments.Currently, he is studying aerospace systems engineering at the University of Akron. He is also a part of the club rugby team at the University of Akron. Schuyler enjoys reading books, especially novels written by Stephen King. His music influence is deeply rooted in motown, funk, rock, and R&B; however, he listens to a wide range of music-- folk, rap, pop punk, indie, house, etc.
