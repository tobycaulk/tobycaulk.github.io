As Time Fades is a four piece pop punk band from Cleveland, Ohio with members Dan Dunlap (Lead Vocals, Rhythm Guitar), Danny Kekedy (Lead Guitar, Backup Vocals), Schuyler Spivey (Bass), and Toby Caulk (Drums).
Their sound is 2000's pop punk with a modern twist. As Time Fades' main influences are Green Day, Slash, We The Kings, Mayday Parade, Neck Deep, My Chemical Romance, and Radiohead. The band appeals to multiple age groups. The lyrics that the band writes adds a connection between the audience and people who have gone through similar situations.
The band has played at big name venues like The Agora Ballroom, The Foundry and The Rock and Roll Hall of Fame. Currently they are working on writing songs for an EP.
